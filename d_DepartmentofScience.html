1. Department of Science

Science department is presently well equipped as per the Board of Technical Education AICTE standards with well established infrastructure apart from the regular activities the department is facilitating the students with technological state of the art at par with the industrial needs. The department has conducted various co-curricular short term training programs to equipped the students with hands on skill at par with the needs of the industries. We wish our students to be competent and also confident to face the interview at this stage. The department has organized various co-curricular programs supported by the association like CET cell. The students are motivated to actively participate in designing of working project model based on the industrial requirement.

Mission :

Our department is well equipped with lab facilities, qualified & dedicated faculty members of Physics, Mathematics and English having specialization in different areas to help and improve the quality & standards of student community. We are also dedicated towards benefit & welfare of the students & to make them strongly fit in the current industry scenario to achieve their goals.

I am confident that our students would justify the credibility of the department by showing a high level of professional competence in their respective job areas.
