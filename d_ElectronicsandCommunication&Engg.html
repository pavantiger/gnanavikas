1. Electronics and Communication & Engineering

The Electronics & Communication Engineering department is presently well equipped as per the Board of Technical Education & AICTE standards with well established infrastructure apart from the regular activities the department is facilitating the students with technological state of the art at par with the industrial needs. The department has conducted various co-curricular short term training programs to equipped the students with hands on skill at par with the needs of the industries. The programs like Embedded System Design, VLSI design & application, computer networking, computer programming and many more are conducted in collaboration with identify organizations. We wish our students to be competent and also confident to face the interview at this stage. The department has organized various co-curricular programs such as Expert lecture, Technical Paper presentation, industrial visit, group discussion, technical quiz the department has organized various co-curricular programs supported by the association like E-Tricks and CET cell. The students are motivated to actively participate in designing of working project model based on the industrial requirement
