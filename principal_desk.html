Education is the key to the resurgence of India. At the present, India needs Technical education of the highest order. The Technical educational system has been subjected to fast, radical and revolutionary changes over recent years. To keep pace with changes, the commitment to high quality of teaching learning with discipline of staff and students is quite essential. It is achieved by excellent academic ambience through conducive environment for studies, State-of-art laboratories, communication centre & digital library.

Our aim is to develop an e-Campus through WI-FI and positive steps are initiated. We are focusing not only on academic front, but on the multifaceted development of students by encouraging them for sports, cultural events,Sports Club, Yoga and other extra curricular activities. Industries visit our campus and train our students. These efforts have resulted in more placements and we are keen to augment them further.

We are confident in our endeavours to deliver high quality technical education to mould the students into professional technocrats and as responsible citizens.
