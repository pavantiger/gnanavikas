1. Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department is presently well equipped as per the Board of Technical Education & AICTE standards with well established infrastructure apart from the regular activities the department is facilitating the students with technological state of the part with the industrial needs. The department has conducted various co-curricular short term training programs to equipped the students with hands on skill at par with the needs of the industries.

We wish our students to be competent and also confident to face the interview at this stage. The department has organized various co-curricular programs such as Expert lecture, Technical Paper presentation, site visit, group discussion, technical quiz. The students are motivated to actively participate in designing of working project model based on the survey requirement.
